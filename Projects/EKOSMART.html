EKOSMART

Technologies used: Unity, C#, Django, Node.js, Handlebars, Marionette, Less.js, Javascript, jQuery, Python

EkoSmart was created to prove that it is possible to spend less energy while not neglecting the comfort of a home. The player controls the robot EKO and helps a neighborhood to manage the energy of housesmake its inhabitants happy. 

I contributed on the development of the project by doing QA, bugfixing and backend administration of the WebApp. I also monitored Google and Unity Analytics for the project and exported data on behalf of the client.

https://app.ekosmart.fr/
