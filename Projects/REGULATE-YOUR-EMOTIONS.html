REGULATE YOUR EMOTIONS SERIES

Technologies used: Django, Node.js, Handlebars, Marionette, Less.js, Javascript, jQuery

"Regulate your emotions" is series of web applications that teaches users to better understand and how to handle to situations related to their workplace

I contributed to the project's development both fronted and backend by creating content like animations, quizzes, and other gamified content, as well as debugging, writing tests, interpreting client's requirements and QA.
