THESIS

Technologies used: Unity3D, C#

My university thesis was a research on an engine providing game genre adaptability. During the gameplay, statistics from the player's behavior are collected to determine the player's preference according to his gameplay.
Once the player's preference is determined, the next zone of the map is seamlessly adapted at runtime to match the genre that the player preference, while also adjusting in a more dynamic way various elements and interactions around the map according to specific statistical values.
This resulted in a unique experience on every run of the game.

In more detail, I designed, programmed and implemented:

  • the adaptability engine
  • the creation of the map
  • the dialogue system
  • the enemies' AI
  • the patrol system
  • the player's interaction system
  • and various other gameplay elements like UI, controller input, etc.
