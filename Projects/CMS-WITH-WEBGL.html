CMS WITH WEBGL

Technologies used: WebGL, Electron, HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL

A CMS enabling the customer to create and edit his own online/offline application according to his needs, including the option to display and manipulate 3D objects while uploading custom textures to showcase their products.

Designed and developed the front-end and back-end of the CMS providing the user the administrator tools to adjust their content while updating the client applications which store the content for offline usage.
