Gameplay Programmer

As a student of Informatics in Greece and then in Spain (with the Erasmus program for a year), I took various courses related to Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems. Then as a developer I worked on serious games, gamification, web applications, asset integration, model optimization, texturing and rendering optimizations for VR, AR and other realtime rendered applications, both for PC and mobile. Then as a Gameplay Programmer I worked on and designed Gameplay systems including the 3C's, development of new online/offline gameplay features, UI, tools development, engine integration and others, while also owning Gameplay systems and managing a Gameplay team. I love games, game development and software development in general.

My experience so far involves:

  • Game Development
  • Asset integration and optimization
  • Software Development
  • Web Development

Taking part in the creation of innovative and fun games, using modern technologies and gadgets, I offer 7+ years of professional experience, my skills and passion for quality games to a demanding and fast-paced environment with an eye on detail and aesthetics.
